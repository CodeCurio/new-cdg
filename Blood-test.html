Blood Test

Price: Varies by test type

Blood tests are essential diagnostic tools that provide valuable insights into your overall health. They are used to diagnose, monitor, and screen for a wide range of conditions, including infections, chronic diseases, and deficiencies.

Common Types of Blood Tests:

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC): Evaluates overall health and detects disorders like anemia and infections.
  • Lipid Profile: Assesses cholesterol and triglyceride levels to gauge heart health.
  • Blood Sugar Test: Measures glucose levels to diagnose diabetes or pre-diabetic conditions.

Regular blood tests are a proactive step in maintaining health and addressing potential issues early. These tests are quick, minimally invasive, and provide comprehensive health evaluations.
