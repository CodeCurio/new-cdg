MRI Scan

Price: ₹3,000–₹10,000 (varies by body part)

An MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) scan uses strong magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of organs and tissues inside the body. Unlike CT scans, it does not use ionizing radiation, making it a safer choice for many patients.

Uses of MRI Scan:

  • Diagnosing brain and spinal cord conditions.
  • Detecting joint and musculoskeletal disorders.
  • Assessing organ function and abnormalities.
  • Guiding treatments for tumors or cancers.

MRI scans provide unparalleled clarity for soft tissue imaging and are invaluable for diagnosing and monitoring complex medical conditions. They are non-invasive and suitable for patients of all ages.
