1. Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Price: ₹300–₹500

The Complete Blood Count (CBC) is a fundamental test that evaluates the overall health and helps in detecting a wide range of conditions, including anemia, infections, and blood disorders. This test measures several vital components of the blood:

  • Red Blood Cells (RBCs): Responsible for carrying oxygen throughout the body. Low levels may indicate anemia, while high levels could point to dehydration or other conditions.
  • White Blood Cells (WBCs): Play a critical role in fighting infections and indicate the status of your immune system. High or low levels can signal infections or immune disorders.
  • Platelets: Essential for blood clotting, their count helps detect clotting disorders or bleeding risks.
  • Hemoglobin: A protein in RBCs that carries oxygen, its level helps in assessing oxygen-carrying capacity and diagnosing anemia.
  • Hematocrit: Reflects the proportion of red blood cells in the blood, useful for diagnosing dehydration or anemia.

The CBC test is not only quick and simple but also provides critical insights into your overall health. It is often used as a routine health check or to monitor the effectiveness of treatments for various medical conditions.
