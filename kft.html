Kidney Function Test (KFT)

Price: ₹700–₹1,200

The Kidney Function Test (KFT) evaluates the health of your kidneys by analyzing key markers in your blood and urine. This test helps diagnose kidney diseases, monitor existing conditions, and evaluate the effects of medications on renal function.

Key Parameters in KFT:

  • Creatinine: Indicates the kidney’s ability to filter waste. Elevated levels may signal kidney damage.
  • Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN): Reflects protein metabolism and renal efficiency.
  • Electrolytes: Includes sodium, potassium, and chloride levels to ensure a balanced metabolic state.
  • Uric Acid: Monitors the kidneys’ ability to excrete waste products.

This test is essential for early detection of chronic kidney disease (CKD), dehydration, and metabolic imbalances. Early diagnosis ensures better management of conditions that could lead to kidney failure if left untreated.
