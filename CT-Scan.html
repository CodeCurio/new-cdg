CT Scan

Price: ₹2,000–₹8,000 (varies by body part)

A CT (Computed Tomography) scan uses X-rays and advanced computer processing to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body. It provides more detailed information than standard X-rays and is used for diagnosing injuries, infections, and diseases.

Applications of CT Scan:

  • Detecting tumors or cancers.
  • Diagnosing fractures or internal injuries.
  • Evaluating heart and lung conditions.
  • Guiding biopsies and surgeries.

CT scans are non-invasive, fast, and provide accurate images that help doctors make better-informed diagnoses. Contrast materials may sometimes be used to enhance the visibility of specific tissues or organs.
